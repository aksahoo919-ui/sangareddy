Bring Krishna Consciousness Home

Host a Program

Invite the holy name into your home, community, colleges or schools — we're happy to arrange a program tailored to your gathering, free of charge.

✦

HOST A HOUSE PROGRAM

Invite our devotees into your home for an evening of kirtan, a short spiritual talk, and prasadam with your family and friends. A wonderful way to bring Krishna consciousness into your household.

REQUEST A PROGRAM →
✦

HOST A KIRTAN

Organize an ecstatic kirtan program at your home, office, or community hall. Our kirtan team brings the instruments, the chanting, and the joy — you just provide the space and the gathering.

REQUEST A KIRTAN →
✦

HOST A COLLEGE PROGRAM

Invite our devotees to your college campus for a short spiritual talk, and prasadam with your fellow students. A wonderful way to bring Krishna consciousness into your academic community.

REQUEST A PROGRAM →
✦

HOST A SCHOOL PROGRAM

Arrange a program for your school for a short spiritual talk, and prasadam with your fellow students. A wonderful way to bring Krishna consciousness into your educational community.
