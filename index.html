About Us

A Sacred Abode of
Sri Sri Jagannath Baladeva Subhadra

ISKCON Sangareddy is a center of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness, founded by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada in 1966.

Located at Vidya Nagar Colony in Sangareddy, Telangana, our temple is dedicated to Sri Sri Jagannath Baladeva Subhadra — the Lord of the Universe, together with His divine siblings. We offer a complete experience of Bhakti Yoga to all who seek divine love and inner peace.
